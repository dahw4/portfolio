About the Project

The K21C clan art series was produced for a competitive gaming community requiring bold, high-impact character and banner artwork. Each piece was created start-to-finish in a single session, exploring a dark, high-contrast aesthetic with heavy line work paired with dramatic ambient lighting.

The work demonstrates proficiency in rapid visual storytelling, digital brush technique, and compositional clarity within tight time constraints. A full speed art timelapse is available on the Speed Art page.
