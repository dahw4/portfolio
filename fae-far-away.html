About the Game

Fae Far Away is a side-scrolling puzzle-platformer that blends atmospheric storytelling and Celtic folklore mythology. The player navigates both Light and Dark planes of the Fae Wilds, using environmental puzzles to restore balance.

Developed collaboratively through Rogue Pigeon Productions, the game targeted Steam and itch.io with a polished vertical slice demo. My scope spanned the entire visual identity of the project.
