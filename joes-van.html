About the Project

Joe's Painting & Decorating required a van livery that would work as both a moving advertisement and a professional calling card. The brief was to create something bold and immediately readable at speed on the road, while communicating the trade clearly.

The concept leans into the craft itself - the contact details are rendered as if painted onto the van with a roller, directly referencing the service being offered. The final logo was colour-matched for vinyl durability and maximum legibility across all lighting conditions.
