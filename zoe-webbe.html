About the Project

Zoe Webbe Hairdressing needed a professional visual identity that felt personal, clean, and premium. The brief centred on creating a mark that would work as both a watermark for photography and as a printed logo for business cards and promotional materials.

The resulting design uses refined typography with a subtle interlocking ZW monogram, flexible enough to sit across photography, social media headers, and printed collateral while maintaining a consistent and elegant impression.
