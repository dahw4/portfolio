K21C Clan Art

A character and banner illustration produced for the K21C gaming community. Speed-painted entirely digitally in Photoshop, demonstrating competency in rapid visual storytelling and digital brush technique under tight deadlines.

The K21C pieces explored a dark, high-contrast aesthetic - heavy line work paired with dramatic ambient lighting. Each piece was produced start-to-finish in a single session.
