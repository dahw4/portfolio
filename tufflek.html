About the Project

Tufflek required a full rebrand as part of a company rename from Croylek. The brief called for a modern, professional identity that would work across physical product applications, including printed bags, vehicle signage, and product labels, while retaining the distinctive flowing T motif from the original mark.

The redesign refined the letterform geometry, updated the colour palette, and developed a flexible system including a primary version, a white-label variant, and a no-background mark for versatile application.
