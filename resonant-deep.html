About the Project

Resonant Deep is an interactive media experience built in Unreal Engine as part of the Scripting for IM module. The project explores how sound and environment design can be driven by scripted systems, creating a space where audio becomes an active gameplay element rather than a passive backdrop.

The project demonstrates a full pipeline from concept through design and implementation, with a focus on scripting logic, player feedback systems, and cohesive audio-visual direction.
